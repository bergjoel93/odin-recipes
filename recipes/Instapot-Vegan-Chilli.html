Instapot Vegan Chili

Description

Instant Pot Vegan Chili made with Black Beans,Quinoa and Sweet Potatoes.This Instant Pot Quinoa Chili is Vegan,Gluten Free & Dairy Free.

Ingredients

  • 1 White Onion
  • 1 to 2 Sweet Potato(s)
  • Avacado to add
  • Cilantro
  • Green onions
  • Sour Cream
  • 28 oz Canned Diced Tomatos
  • 16 oz Canned Black Beans
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 2 Tbs Chili powder
  • 2 tsp Cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp salt
  • Shredded Cheese
  • 1 Tbs olive oil
  • 2 cups of water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat instapot on Saute mode and add olive oil.
  2. Add diced onions and salt and pepper. Let cook.
  3. Add in cubed sweet potato
  4. Turn off saute mode after about 5 minutes of cooking and add the cans of tomatos, beans, and garlic. Add spices and 2 cups of water.
  5. Pressure cook on high for 8 minutes.
  6. Release the steam. Optional: use hand blender to blend the soup to make it more thick and creamy.
  7. Add toppings and enjoy!
